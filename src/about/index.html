Howdy! This is Sunshine Park, a personal website.

This park is still under construction, so make sure to follow the path markers since there's still some dead ends. We have water fountains to help you stay hydrated. You can also eat at the picnic tables if you like, but don't feed the animals.

I'm webmaster for this site, or shall I say, park ranger. I enjoy science, especially entomology and alternative-energy related topics. Not that this site will talk about alternative energy. I also enjoy crochet. You can look at the projects I've completed here.

This website was built as a practice of coding and as a way of "self-expression" (whatever that means). This site is likely to be unfinished for a while. I intially coded this website on Notepad++, VS 2022, and now VS Codium (I'm indecisive, hah). Most images were found on Unsplash and are in the creative commmmons, except the crochet photos. You can find citations for (some) of the images in the resources page.
